1 Binary Tree.

A tree is a basic and widely used data structure, there is multiple types of trees. I will only focus on binary trees here.

1.1 Structure:

A tree consists of elements called Nodes organized in hierarchical arrangement. The node at the top of the hierarchy is called the Root. The nodes directly below the root are the children, which in turn have children of their own.
